Food Destinations

Explore India's most delicious cities and their signature dishes

Old Delhi

Explore the narrow lanes of Chandni Chowk, where the air is thick with the aroma of sizzling parathas, spicy chaat, and sweet jalebis.

Specialty: Chole Bhature & Parathas

Kolkata

The city of joy offers exquisite Bengali cuisine, from street-side puchkas to elaborate fish curries and iconic sweets like rasgulla.

Specialty: Macher Jhol & Rosogolla

Mumbai

From vada pav to bhel puri, Mumbai's street food scene is legendary. Don't miss the coastal delicacies and Irani cafes.

Specialty: Vada Pav & Seafood

Featured Recipe

Butter Chicken - A North Indian Classic

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

A rich and creamy curry that's beloved worldwide. This recipe stays true to its Delhi origins while being accessible for home cooks.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste

About Varsha

Hello! I'm Varsha, a food enthusiast and travel blogger with a passion for exploring India's diverse culinary landscape.

Growing up in a Bengali family in Kolkata, I developed an early appreciation for regional flavors. My journey began in my grandmother's kitchen, where I learned traditional recipes that have been passed down through generations.

Through this blog, I share my culinary adventures across India - from street food stalls in Mumbai to royal kitchens in Rajasthan. Join me as we explore the stories behind India's most beloved dishes!
